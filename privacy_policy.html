Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 27, 2026

LuminaCast ("we", "our", or "us") is a professional projection mapping application developed by İsmet Ünal. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

LuminaCast is designed to work primarily on-device. We collect minimal data:

  • App preferences – Language selection, onboarding status, and Pro subscription state are stored locally on your device using system storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Purchase information – In-app purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • Network discovery – When scanning for projectors, the app broadcasts on your local Wi-Fi network. No data is sent to external servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or tracking libraries.
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV) for advertising purposes.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

3. In-App Purchases

LuminaCast offers optional Pro subscriptions (Monthly, Annual) and a one-time Lifetime purchase. All transactions are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time through your Apple ID settings.

4. Local Network Usage

LuminaCast requires local network access to discover and connect to projectors on the same Wi-Fi network. This communication happens entirely within your local network and is not transmitted to any server.

5. Data Storage

All app data (preferences, project files, calibration settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate cloud servers or sync your data remotely.

6. Children's Privacy

LuminaCast is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
